# Vee Labs

Mobile apps, security tools, and open-source experiments.

---

## Projects

### [BreachGuard](https://github.com/vee-labs/BreachGuard)
Privacy-first password and email breach monitor for Android and iOS.  
Uses k-anonymity — passwords are never transmitted.  
**Flutter · Firebase · HaveIBeenPwned · RevenueCat**

---

### [ACServiceApp](https://github.com/vee-labs/ACServiceApp)
Field service ticketing app for AC technicians.  
Role-based access for Admin, Office Staff, and Technicians. Real-time Firestore sync.  
**Kotlin · Android · Firebase · MVVM**
